    Honesty boundary

    This tool is the intake acceptance harness. It does not mint Genesis shards or sign anything. Surfaces that pass here are candidates for custody, not truth. Promotion into sealing and detached verification happens in the AXM kernel after intake.

    A missing field does not kill the analysis. It lowers the evidence tier or becomes a declared gap. Human review routes attention; it does not convert weak evidence into strong evidence.

    What it computes

    The browser hashes the supplied bytes, maps familiar field names including Air Force vocabulary such as NIIN, JCN, tail number, AWP/NMCS, and EDD, joins blocked work to the required part, and separates administrative on-hand from serviceable-and-unallocated stock.

    Where the recorded state and the physically supportable state diverge, the divergence is the finding.

    Where it sits

    AXM is the governance layer between recorded state and consequential action: the part that checks whether a premise is physically supportable before planning speed magnifies the error. Accepted surfaces can move into custody, sealing, detached verification, query, and replay without changing the source system.
