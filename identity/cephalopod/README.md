# Cephalopod identity research

This directory is an incubator for a separate cephalopod mark. It is not part of the frozen SCG dandelion lineage, and nothing here is a sanctioned identity source until a later freeze explicitly says so.

The working concept is an anti-fragile cephalopod architecture: a coherent central organism distributes sensing and action across semi-autonomous arms, exploits unstable substrate, and converts orderly environmental failure into additional routes, braces, openings, and contact points.

Research lives under `research/`. Candidate proof sheets are evidence of exploration, not source maps. A selected mark will require its own canonical bitmap map, palette tokens, integer-scale exports, scale-specific small states, provenance record, and coexistence law before it can be treated as governed identity.
